Untitled (l’homme dans un jeu de sphères)
c.1930

Lithograph on paper
Printed by Mourlot Freres, Paris
Published in Arts et Metiers Graphiques, issue 18 (July 1930)

This conceptual illustration accompanied an article about modern printing technologies. Brodovitch contributed two unique lithographs to this issue of Arts et Metiers Graphiques; the other work, Untitled (Graphisme), follows on the timeline.
