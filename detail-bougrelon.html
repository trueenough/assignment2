Monsieur de Bougrelon
1928

Engravings on paper
Published by Henri Jonquières, Paris
(from Les Beaux Romans series)
Edition of 1050 (#669)
Text by Jean Lorrain

This work contains eighteen highly detailed full-page black and white engravings. The imagery suggests influences from Cubist and Surrealist artists on Brodovitch’s work.
