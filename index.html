Brodovitch
Beyond Bazaar

Best known for his 24-year tenure as the groundbreaking art director of Harper’s Bazaar (1934-58), Russian emigre Alexey Brodovitch began his career as a prolific painter, photographer, illustrator, and commercial artist. Through this archive, you can now explore the lesser-known work of this visionary artist.
